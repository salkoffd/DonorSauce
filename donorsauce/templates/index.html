Where does the money come from?

Political campaigns cost millions of dollars. In 2018, over $5.7 billion was spent on congressional elections. Politicians are often times pressured to change their platforms to match donor interests. This project attemps to clarify political contributions: who receives money, who supplies it, and how much?


How to use this website

This dataset can be viewed in several ways. Want to know your representative's donors? Head over to the maps page. Want to know which legislators receive money from a specific corporation? Select "Donors" from the Data drop-down menu and search for a corporation. Select "Summary" for overall statistics.
